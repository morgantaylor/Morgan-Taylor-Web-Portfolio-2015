Study 4 - Responsive Art Blog

This is my favorite art blog that I redesigned.

Objective:

I redesigned this art blogs website to be fully responsive. The design needed to be clean and simple to allow the user to interact with and find what they want without feeling overwhelmed.

Wireframes

Challenge:

Bringing art to the masses. In my experience I'm more likely to look at blogs while on the go on a mobile device. I wanted to make a responsive design that would showcase the art and the artist most importantly while keeping out the clutter. What is important to view on a larger screen changes when viewing on other devices.

Mockups

Solution:

Colorful and whimsical colors with very clear fonts to aid all devices. Advertisements can be seen on some devices but go away with smaller devices to allow the art to have more visibility.

Learned:

I learned how to create a fully responsive website using the Bootstrap framework. Problem solving was a huge factor in this project. Some issues addressed were drop down menus, fixed navigation bars, and fluid rows/containers.
