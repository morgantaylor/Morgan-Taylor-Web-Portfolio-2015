Study 2 - Food Recipe Website

Objective:

Project guidelines were to create a wireframe and mockup of a 5 page or more website that highlights best practices in user interface design.

Wireframes

Challenge:

I chose to design a food/recipe website for the challenge of working with large amounts of visual information. The goal for me was to keep users from getting lost. A user should be able to know where they want to go, what information they want to find, then find it without jumping through any hoops.

Mockups

Solution:

In researching multiple recipe websites I came up with this design. I utilized bread crumb navigation to solve the problem of users getting lost, and a rating system to help users engage with their content.

Final Design:

I like working with white and grey. It is very simple and allows for other elements to stand out on their own. I accompanied them with shades and tints of the brand color red.
With the amount of information that needed to be displayed, the structure of the information was designed to guide the eye down the page while giving users clear and precise options on where to navigate next.
